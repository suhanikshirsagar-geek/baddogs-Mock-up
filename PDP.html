Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find a specific item? +
Use the search field to enter the title, keyword, or item number. You can also browse by collection or contact our team directly.
Does the image accurately represent what I'll receive? +
Our website images come directly from our print files. Since screens are backlit, colours may appear slightly different. Physical pieces often look even better in person.
How accurate are the listed dimensions? +
Listed sizes reflect outside frame dimensions. Variations are minimal — no more than 1/8" (0.125"). Contact us for exact specifications.
Is my package insured? +
Yes. We guarantee your satisfaction and will replace any lost or damaged artwork. Photograph any damage on arrival and contact us — we'll ship a replacement within 72 hours.
What is the difference between Canvas and Paper? +
Canvas prints are gallery-wrapped and ready to hang. Paper editions are giclée on 300gsm cotton rag — they require framing and are recommended with an archival mat.
How do I contact you for urgent requests? +
Email info@baddogeditions.com or call (407) 826-1197. We respond within 24 hours on weekdays. Include your order number and photographs for urgent replacements.
